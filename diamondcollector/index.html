The Game

Diamondcollector is a game in the genre "idle-gaming" and the goal is to have as much money / ores as possible. The concept of Diamondcollector is that you are in control of a mining company which is slowly growing and increasing its profits. Buying upgrades is like hiring workers or machinery that work for you so you don't have too get your hands dirty (by clicking).

How to play

Click the big rock to manually mine ores, this is the first step to be able to hire workers that does this for you. Sell your gained ores to collect money. Keep an eye on the crafting tab and see if there is something you need a specific ore to craft. For example the Sand pickaxe. This craftable increases your clickingpower and every click will earn you double the ores! This is a good first item to craft.

When you have enough money for the first upgrade you can start idling. This means that workers will earn you a specific clicks per second (CPS) while you just sit back and relax. Of course getting your hands dirty will give you more income but it is up too you if you want to do this or wait while your workers make you rich.

The progress tab is a level indicator, levels are earned by experience workers which in turn is gained by reseting your game. Experience workers costs 1,000,000,000 money each and gives you 1 experience / second. When you reset your game you only keep your experience workers and levels so make sure it is worth it!
